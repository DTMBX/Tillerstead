Standards & Compliance

Built to TCNA Standards, Licensed in NJ

We follow the Tile Council of North America (TCNA) Handbook and applicable ANSI standards (A108, A118) on every project—not because homeowners ask for it, but because it's the right way to build bathrooms that last. We document it so you have proof the work meets code and performs as intended.

Do you build full shower waterproofing systems or just tile?

We build complete wet-area assemblies—prep, waterproofing, drains/transitions, then tile—so performance isn’t dependent on “surface fixes.”

Do you do flood testing?

When the project scope and site conditions allow, flood testing is used to verify the pan/assembly before tile goes in.

What areas do you serve?

Primary service area: Atlantic, Ocean, and Cape May Counties. If you’re nearby, reach out and we’ll confirm availability.
