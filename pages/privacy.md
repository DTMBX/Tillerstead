## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2025  

---

## 1. Who we are

Tillerstead LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is a licensed and insured contractor serving South Jersey.  
We operate [tillerstead.com](https://tillerstead.com) and collect only the information needed to:

- Respond to inquiries  
- Schedule work  
- Run our business responsibly  

---

## 2. Information we collect

- **Contact details** – your name, phone number, email, and project address.  
- **Project details** – descriptions, measurements, photos, timelines, and budget goals you provide.  
- **Technical data** – IP address, browser type, and device data collected by hosting or basic analytics tools.  

---

## 3. How we use your information

- To respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, and provide estimates.  
- To plan, perform, document, and invoice contracting services.  
- To improve our website, messaging, and service processes.  
- To comply with New Jersey licensing, insurance, and record-keeping requirements.  

We **do not sell or trade personal data**. Information may be shared only with service providers  
(such as hosting, email, CRM, payment, or financing partners) when needed to fulfill your request  
and always in a way intended to comply with applicable privacy laws.

---

## 4. Cookies & analytics

This site may use basic cookies and analytics to understand traffic and performance.

- Cookies are optional and can be disabled in your browser settings.  
- Disabling cookies will not prevent you from reading the site.  
- Some form or interactive functionality may be limited if cookies or scripts are blocked.

---

## 5. Data retention

We retain contact and project details only as long as necessary to:

- Respond to your requests  
- Maintain accurate service records  
- Meet legal, tax, or insurance obligations  

Routine backups and log files may be kept for a limited time for security and troubleshooting.

---

## 6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information submitted through our forms and communications.  
However, no online transmission or storage system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee  
absolute protection against threats beyond our control.

---

## 7. Your choices

- You can ask us to update or delete your contact details.  
- You can opt out of non-essential emails at any time.  
- You can disable cookies in your browser settings.  

Where applicable law gives you additional rights (such as access or correction), we will make a good-faith effort to honor those requests.

---

## 8. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services or legal requirements change.

- The **“Last updated”** date will reflect the most recent revision.  
- Continued use of the site after changes are posted indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
