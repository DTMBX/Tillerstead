Why This Process Matters

Problems Show Up Before Tile

Flatness issues, deflection, moisture exposure, and drain placement all determine whether a tile assembly succeeds. Catching and correcting these items before tile prevents expensive tear-outs later.

Verification Beats Assumptions

Thinset doesn’t fix bad prep, and grout doesn’t stop water. Readiness checks and system verification reduce the risk of cracked tile, leaks, and premature failure.

Documentation Protects Everyone

Photo documentation and written scope protect you, the installer, and future owners by showing what was built— not just what it looks like after completion.
