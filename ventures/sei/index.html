Why SweatEquity Insurance?

Transparent pricing, safe-driver rewards, and member-aligned incentives

100% Transparent Pricing

See exactly where your premium goes: risk pool, operating costs, reinsurance, and member benefits. No hidden fees, no black-box calculations.

  • Real-time premium allocation dashboard
  • Double-entry accounting ledger
  • Export court-ready financial statements

Safe-Driver Rewards

Your driving behavior directly impacts your score and member benefit accrual. Explainable AI scoring with transparent factors you can improve.

  • Phone sensor integration (no hardware)
  • Speed, braking, night driving, mileage factors
  • Score history & improvement recommendations

Court-Ready Compliance

Built for regulatory scrutiny from day one. Immutable audit logs, state-by-state configuration, and compliance mode toggles.

  • SHA-256 immutable audit trail
  • Rewards-only / benefit ledger mode toggle
  • PDF exports for legal proceedings

Technology Architecture

Backend
