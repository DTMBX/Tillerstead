How Long Does a Bathroom Remodel Take?

Typical timeline: 2-3 weeks. Demo (1-2 days), plumbing/electrical (2-3 days), waterproofing (2-3 days), tile installation (3-5 days), finishing (2-3 days).

What Should a Bathroom Remodel Cost?

South Jersey average: $8,000-$25,000. Depends on size, materials, and scope. Basic refresh vs. full gut differs significantly.

Do I Need a Permit in NJ?

Usually YES for bathroom remodels involving plumbing or structural changes. Requirements vary by municipality in Atlantic, Ocean, and Cape May Counties.

Curbless Shower vs. Traditional: Which is Right?

Curbless showers offer accessibility, modern look, easier cleaning. Require proper slope (1/4" per foot) and waterproofing. Not ideal for small bathrooms.

TCNA = Tile Council of North America. Sets industry standards for proper tile installation methods, substrate prep, and waterproofing.
