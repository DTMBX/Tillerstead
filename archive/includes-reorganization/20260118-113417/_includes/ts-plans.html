Property Maintenance

Owner-Operated Maintenance Plans for South Jersey Homes

I’m a one-man contractor. That means you get the same person every visit—consistent eyes on your home, honest prioritization, and documented service notes you can keep. Plans are flexible: monthly, seasonal, or tailored to your property and budget.

Maintenance plans focus on inspection, prevention, and small repairs. Larger repairs or remodel scopes are quoted separately with a written proposal.

{% include ts-icon.html name="icon-plan" size="22" class="ts-icon" %} Core Visit Plan

Recurring checkups and small fixes to keep the home stable—especially helpful for busy homeowners, rentals, and seasonal properties.

  • Visual checks for leaks, staining, ventilation issues, and seasonal wear
  • Basic touch-ups and minor repairs when feasible during the visit
  • Clear “now vs. later” recommendations to prevent surprise expenses

{% include ts-icon.html name="icon-concierge" size="22" class="ts-icon" %} Seasonal / Shore Add-Ons

Optional support for storm prep, arrivals, and property readiness—built around your checklist and how you use the home.

  • Storm prep and post-weather checks (photos provided)
  • Key coordination and access notes (by agreement)
  • Basic readiness tasks: supplies, minor hardware, small punch items
